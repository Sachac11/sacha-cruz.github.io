
# Product Support & Backlog Prioritization

## Overview
Improved production support processes and backlog management for enterprise applications supporting over 4,000 users.

## Problem
- Large backlog (300+ items) with unclear prioritization
- Slow defect resolution impacting users
- Inefficient ticket tracking and reporting processes

## Role
Technical Product Owner responsible for backlog ownership and production support management.

## Approach
- Reduced backlog by 40% through structured prioritization strategies
- Improved defect resolution time by approximately 20%
- Implemented a ServiceNow-based ticketing structure for tracking, reporting, and metrics
- Improved communication between support and engineering teams

## Technical Concepts
- Backlog management
- Production support workflows
- Ticketing systems (ServiceNow)

## Outcome
- 40% reduction in backlog size
- Faster defect resolution (~20% improvement)
- Improved system stability and operational visibility
